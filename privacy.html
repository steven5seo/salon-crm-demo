Privacy Notice — Demo Experience

Last updated: March 1, 2025

This website is a simulated marketing and CRM demonstration for a salon franchise. It is designed for educational and sales purposes only.

Data sources

All data displayed within the experience is fictional and stored locally in your browser session. Seeded data is loaded from static JSON files bundled with the site.

Lead capture form

When you submit the lead form, the information you enter is saved only to your browser’s localStorage under the key demo_leads. No submission is transmitted to a server or third party.

To clear the data, open your browser console and run localStorage.removeItem('demo_leads') or clear site data in your browser settings.

Analytics

We include an optional Google Analytics 4 (GA4) snippet for demo measurement. The measurement ID is configurable in data/config.json. By default, only anonymous interaction events (lead_submit, segment_apply, campaign_preview) are tracked, without personally identifiable information.

Disable analytics by setting "enableAnalytics": false in data/config.json. GA4 scripts will not load when disabled.

Cookies & storage

  • No cookies are set by this demo experience.
  • LocalStorage is used solely for the optional lead capture walkthrough.
  • Static assets are hosted on GitHub Pages and served over HTTPS.

Your responsibilities

Avoid entering real customer data. This environment is not intended for production use or storing personal information.

Questions about this demo? Email demo@elevatecrm.com.
